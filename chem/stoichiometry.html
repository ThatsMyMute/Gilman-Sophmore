Key Concepts

  • Mole Ratios: Derived from balanced chemical equations.
  • Limiting Reactant: Determines the maximum amount of product.
  • Theoretical Yield: Maximum amount of product based on the limiting reactant.

Practice Problem

If 10.0 g of H2 reacts with 50.0 g of O2, calculate the amount of H2O produced:

\[ 2H_2 + O_2 \rightarrow 2H_2O \]

Practice Questions

  1. What is the limiting reactant in the reaction of 5.0 g of CH4 with 10.0 g of O2?
  2. Calculate the molar mass of NaCl.
  3. Determine the theoretical yield of CO2 when 10.0 g of C reacts with excess O2.
