Theories of Heat

  • Caloric Theory: Heat is a fluid-like substance that flows from hot to cold objects.
  • Kinetic Molecular Theory: Heat is the motion of particles, and faster-moving particles correspond to higher temperatures.

Heat Transfer

  • Conduction: Transfer of heat through direct contact.
  • Convection: Transfer of heat in fluids due to particle movement.
  • Radiation: Transfer of heat via electromagnetic waves (e.g., sunlight).
